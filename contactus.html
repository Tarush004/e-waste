Awareness and Education:

Raise awareness among students, faculty, and staff about the environmental impact of electronic waste. Conduct workshops, seminars, and awareness campaigns to educate the campus community about proper e-waste disposal practices.


Collection and Recycling Programs:

Establish e-waste collection points on campus where students and staff can drop off their old electronic devices. Collaborate with certified e-waste recycling companies to ensure proper disposal and recycling of electronic waste.
